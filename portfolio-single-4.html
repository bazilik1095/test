Mamalu Studio

Project Details

Date:
Jule 10th, 2020
Client:
Mamalu
Services:
Branding, User Interface Design, User Experience Design, Figma Design, Development
Description:
Implementation:

As a UI/UX designer, I began by conducting extensive research into Mamalù's brand and target audience. This involved understanding the values of hospitality, comfort, passion, and tradition that define Mamalù Studios and Suites.

I crafted a user-centric design that prioritized ease of navigation and accessibility. This design incorporated modern elements, such as a Self Check-in feature and smart technologies throughout the structure, to ensure a stress-free and contemporary guest experience.

The website was meticulously designed, ensuring that it was responsive across various devices. Visual elements, typography, and color schemes were carefully chosen to evoke the warmth and uniqueness of Mamalù Studios and Suites.

Results:

The website successfully captures the essence of Mamalù's hospitality and Napoli's traditions. It serves as a modern, user-friendly platform that allows guests to explore and engage with Mamalù effortlessly. The incorporation of smart technologies has streamlined the guest experience, making it more convenient.

The project has contributed to increased online visibility for Mamalù and has attracted a broader audience of both leisure and business travelers. The website now stands as a testament to Mamalù's commitment to passion and excellence in the heart of Napoli.
